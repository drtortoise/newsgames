London NewsGames meetup

If you work in or around newsrooms and make news-games or would like to make newsgames, this is the meetup for you.

This edition of our newsgames meetup is going to be a special on Syria. We have Mamdouh Akbiek and Eloise Dicker from the BBC talking about Syrian Journey, Harriet Grant from the Guardian talking about Fortress Europe, and Dr Tomas Rawlings from GameTheNews (an initiative of Auroch Digital) talking about Endgame:Syria. Get a ticket.

Time

Monday October 19th, 6pm to 8.30pm

Location

The Guardian, Kings Place, 90 York Way, King's Cross, London N1 9GU

The Guardian office is located at Kings Place, just 150 metres from King's Cross and St Pancras Stations, one of the most connected locations in London and now the biggest transport hub in Europe. We'll be on the 2nd floor in the Morning Conference room. Take the elevator up to the 1st floor reception to check in. There will be light snacks and tea/coffee. (No alcohol however, know any one who would like to sponsor?)
