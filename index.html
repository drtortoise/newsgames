London NewsGames meetup

If you work in or around newsrooms and make news-games or would like to make newsgames, this is the meetup for you. The porposed agenda for this first meetup is a short talk about an actual newsgame, followed by discussion and deconstruction of the game machanics involved, then getting hands-on with some paper prototyping. This time we're lucky to have Adiprat Virdi present the BBC's Syrian Journey. Adiprat can't make it, Paul Rissen from the BBC will be leading the event instead. Get a ticket.

Time

May 26th, 6pm to 8.30pm

Location

Top Office Machines, 133-135 Bethnal Green Road, E2 7DG, London

Top Office Machines is in hipstery Shoreditch, just moments away from Brick Lane and that cat cafe.
