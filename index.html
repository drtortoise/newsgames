London NewsGames meetup

If you work in or around newsrooms and make news-games or would like to make newsgames, this is the meetup for you. The porposed agenda for this first meetup is a short talk about an actual newsgame, followed by discussion and deconstruction of the game machanics involved, then getting hands-on with some paper prototyping. Get a ticket.

Time

April 14th, 6pm to 9pm

Location

101 St. Martins Lane (3rd Floor), London, WC2N 4AZ

MozSpace can be found in the heart of London just round the corner from Leicester square and Covent Garden. A modern space with a bright orange bar well stocked with snacks, soda, tea and coffee. (No alcohol however, know any one who would like to sponsor?)

Need help getting to the venue? Check out this video from Christian Heilmann on how to get to MozSpace London from Leicester Square tube station (2 min 17 sec).
